## Training Philosophy

### Core Principle: Consistency Over Complexity

- **Full body first.** Every new client starts with a full body program, 3 days/week. Learn the movements, build the habit, see results.
- **Same exercises for 6-8 weeks.** Exercises don't need to change - people adapt slower than they think, especially beginners. Constant program hopping is why people fail.
- **Earn complexity.** Splits (upper/lower, PPL) are earned through 6-8 weeks of consistent adherence, not given on day one.
- **Simple nutrition.** Cook → Measure → Eat. No MyFitnessPal. No calorie tracking apps. Portion-based system with macro targets.
- **No fake timelines.** Fat loss happens through consistency. No "lose 20 lbs in 30 days" promises. The program works - the variable is the client showing up.

### Why This Works (Market Validation)

- PAA data shows "Do 90% of people quit the gym after 3 months?" is a real search query
- People quit because programs get too complicated, not because they're too simple
- The #1 PAA across every city: "Is a personal trainer worth it?" → The answer is yes, IF the system is simple enough to stick with
