# Eccentric Iron Fitness - Keyword Research Report
**Date:** March 15, 2026
**Source:** DataForSEO (Canada / British Columbia)

---

## Executive Summary

The calculator opportunity is MASSIVE. "TDEE calculator" pulls 49,500 searches/month in Canada with LOW competition and a difficulty of only 28. "Macro calculator" adds another 9,900/mo at difficulty 42. Combined, that's nearly 60,000 monthly searches for tools you already have built. This is the #1 traffic driver for the site.

City-specific personal trainer searches are modest (90-320/mo per city) but have high intent and low competition. The PAA questions are almost identical across all cities - pricing, session frequency, and "is it worth it?" - which means one strong FAQ/content strategy covers the entire Lower Mainland.

People searching for nutrition want **structured variety** - specific diet frameworks (Mediterranean, Keto), time-bound plans (7-day), and goal-oriented guidance (weight loss, body recomp). Not "simple nutrition."

### The Answer: People Want Structured Variety

The data clearly shows:
1. **Named diet frameworks dominate** - Mediterranean (4,400), Keto (1,600), DASH (1,600). People want a system with a name.
2. **Time-bound plans are huge** - "7-day meal plan" appears in 6+ keyword variations totaling ~9,600 searches
3. **Goal-specific** - Weight loss meal plans, diabetic meal plans, fat loss - they search by outcome
4. **NOT searching for "simple meals"** - No significant volume for "easy nutrition" or "simple diet"

**For Phase 2 (Web App):** This validates building a meal planner with variety. People don't want to eat chicken and rice every day. They want a structured system that gives them diverse options within their macro targets.

## 6. Key Takeaways

1. **The calculator IS the business.** 60,000+ monthly searches with low competition. This single page can drive more traffic than everything else combined.

2. **PAA questions are your FAQ goldmine.** The same pricing and "is it worth it" questions appear everywhere. Answer them once, use them on every page, win rich snippets.

3. **City pages are table stakes, not traffic drivers.** 90-320 searches/mo per city. Essential for local SEO and Google Business Profile, but the real volume comes from calculator + blog.

4. **"Female personal trainer" is a real search.** Every city shows this as a related search. Your fiancee's trainer profile directly addresses this demand.

5. **People want structured variety, not simple nutrition.** Mediterranean, keto, 7-day plans - they want a framework with diverse options. This validates the meal planner app for Phase 2.

6. **Review count matters.** Top competitors have 100-639 reviews. Google Business Profile optimization and review generation should be a priority.

7. **Low difficulty blog topics exist.** "Counting macros for beginners" (difficulty 6) and "counting macros for dummies" (difficulty 6) are essentially free rankings that funnel directly to your calculator.
